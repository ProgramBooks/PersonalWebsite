My Programming Introduction

I was first introduced to computer programming through a Khan Academy program my mom made me take in school. I was convinced I would hate programming, but I started doing the course, because I had to. And I loved it. Every morning, I started my school day with my assigned coding for the day. I didn't think much of how I enjoyed the program, until christmas break when my sister and mom started talking about how I loved coding and it could be something I could do for a job. That was in 8th grade. I have been programming and learning about women in tech ever since.
