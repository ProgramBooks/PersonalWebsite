The Basics

My name is Lily Thomas. I am 16 years old. I am a rising junior, and have been homeschooled my whole life. I like math and reading. If I could read a new book every day I would. I have four older sisters who I look up to and admire. I have lived in Kennedale Texas my whole life. My favorite thing about computers is that you can do anything with a computer. The possibilities are endless. I want my life to be a little like a computer. I never want to limit myself. I want to do things no one thought I could.

Hobbies

I love to read, dance, and bake. I love reading because books help me understand my world. While some people might fail to see the similarity between Harry Potter's Hogwarts and Fort Worth Texas, I find that characters and fantasy lands have the ability to take complex world issues and break them down to simple elements. I also love diving into a intriguing story when I feel stressed about things in my life. I love to dance because dancing gives me the ability to express myself by moving around and it doesn't have to be graceful to be amazing. I love the simplicity and completity of dancing. While those may seem to conflict each other, I find that they actually compliment each other in dance. Baking is an art and a tasty way to express yourself. I love the challanges of baking and I love that it isn't as hard as most people think it is. Anyone can learn to bake and there is something for everyone to enjoy in baking.
